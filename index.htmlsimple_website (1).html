Privacy Policy

Ben Home Health Care LLC is committed to protecting the privacy of our patients, clients, and website visitors. This website is intended for informational purposes only.

We do not collect personal health information (PHI) through this website. Any personal information voluntarily submitted through email or phone communication is used solely to respond to inquiries and coordinate services.

We implement reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect any information shared with us. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties except as required by law.

HIPAA Disclaimer

Ben Home Health Care LLC complies with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Protected Health Information (PHI) is handled in accordance with federal privacy and security regulations.

Please do not submit confidential medical information through this website or via unsecured email. For matters involving patient care or medical information, contact us directly by phone.

Our staff is trained to maintain the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all patient health information as required by HIPAA.
